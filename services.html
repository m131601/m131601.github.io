Consulting Services

Managing construction projects requires significant time and investment, making skilled oversight crucial for ensuring that projects are completed on schedule, within budget, and to high-quality standards. In the competitive building and construction industry, companies must excel in coordinating job sites, controlling costs, and effectively managing risks to remain cost-effective and competitive. P&C Custom Homes’ highly trained Construction Project Managers are exceptionally qualified to provide the oversight necessary to ensure your projects are delivered on time and meet your expected standards for quality, scope, and functionality. Our experts understand the challenges faced by owners, contractors, engineers, and architects throughout the construction process. To address your specific project needs, P&C Custom Homes offers the following construction consulting services:

Consulting Services – Oversight

The requirements for oversight in construction consulting can vary significantly based on the complexity of the project, its importance, political sensitivities, public interest, and the competence of the contractor. Our services encompass everything from feasibility studies to the development and maintenance of project schedules and budgets, as well as the management of construction contracts and change orders. P&C Custom Homes’ consulting division is dedicated to providing the necessary level of attention and oversight your project requires.

Feasibility Assessment

P&C Custom Homes conducts a thorough feasibility assessment to evaluate the accuracy and completeness of construction plans and specifications while also validating schedules and budgets. This comprehensive review checks for document completeness, identifies inconsistencies, potential liabilities, coordination issues, and overlooked details. Additionally, it aims to clarify ambiguous contract language that could result in unnecessary change orders, disputes, and strained relationships among contractors, owners, designers, and project stakeholders

Contractual Management

Contract and procurement management are crucial services provided by P&C Custom Homes at the commencement of any project or program. Our capabilities include revising contract documents, crafting requests for proposals, and leading the evaluation and selection of contractors. We also play a key role in developing a procurement strategy that leverages advantageous market conditions while preserving the overall project timeline. By focusing on these activities during the initial stages of a project’s lifecycle, P&C Custom Homes can greatly enhance the project’s value.

Schedule Strategy Development

P&C Custom Homes is committed to ensuring projects remain on schedule. We are experts in developing, reviewing, and managing design and construction timelines. Our team sets schedules during the design phase to outline the initial timeline, overall project duration, and key tasks. Throughout the construction process, we utilize these schedules to track progress, identify changes and delays, and evaluate their impact on project completion. After construction, we apply these schedules to assist with project closeout and assess any claims that may come up.
